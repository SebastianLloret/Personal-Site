Language

I'm an amateur linguist with native fluency in English and Spanish, conversational fluency in French, and a basic grasp of German. I think languages are a beautiful window into different cultures, and one of the easiest ways to broaden one's own perspective.

Coding

Photography

If I'm not locked indoors, chances are you'll catch me taking advantage of Boulder's beautiful scenery and night life. I use a Nikon D7100, and keep some of my work on my Instagram.

Activism

You'll probably catch me at environmental protests throughout the state, especially Keep Colorado Green events.
